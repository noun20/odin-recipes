Tasty Lasagna!

Description

This is the best most amzing delicious lasagna recipe that you wil ever taste. Be prepared for your whole family to love you so much for cooking them this most amzing recipe. If music be the food of love then play on.

You will start by creating your own lasagna noods. Then you will create the perfect sauce, sweet yet sophisticated, simple and loving. Finally you will curd your own cheese and slaughter your own cow to have the freshest meat. This will be a meal you'll never forget. Please follow along and try for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup sliced olives
  • 1 pinch basil
  • 1 tablespoon driued oregeno
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried sage
  • 2 (14ounce) jars marinara sauce
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese shredded

Directions

  1. Mix together ingredients in an oven pan
  2. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees F
  3. Bake for 45 minutes and check 30 mins in to see if the color of cheese is changing brown
  4. If cheese is golden brown, remove from oven and enjoy!
