Air Fryer Meatloaf

Description

This is the best most amzing delicious Air Fryer Meatloaf recipe that you wil ever taste. Be prepared for your whole family to love you so much for cooking them this most amazing recipe. If music be the food of love then play on.

This simple air fryer meatloaf is easy to make and ready to eat in less than an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 1 green pepper chopped
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt n pepper
  • 2 (14ounce) jars tomato sauce

Directions

  1. Mix together ingredients in an oven pan
  2. Pre heat air fryer to 375 degrees F
  3. Bake for 45 minutes and check 30 mins in to see if the color of the meatloaf is changing brown
  4. If center of meatloaf is brown, remove from oven and enjoy!
